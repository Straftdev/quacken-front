Capture the flag! close

This game mode has two main variations. Either take the flags back to your spawn zone, or to your base to defend.

The flags

The flags here work different than blockade flags. You pick them up by driving over them and drop them by using the flag maneuver. 1 point flags have to be dropped in the 8 tiles surrounding a beacon (3 point flag) matching your team color to earn points. 2 point flags need to be taken back to your spawn zone. 3 point flags cannot be picked up.

New Boats

  • Healer - Healing shot, heal self.
  • Sniper - Long shot, double forward.
  • Bomber - Place bomb, torpedo.
  • Builder - Place rock, in-place turn.

New Maneuvers

  • Healing shot - Heals 25% (50% golden)
  • Heal self - Heals 25% (50% golden)
  • Place rock - Places a short rock on an empty tile next to the boat. Golden can drop on a wind as well. If you hit a boat, it sinks instantly.
  • Place bomb - Place a bomb similar to kraken hunt, but it moves with winds and whirls. Deals 50% damage (75% golden).
  • Torpedo - Fires a torpedo from the front of your ship which travels 2 tiles and removes short rocks in a 3x3 area. It will also clear other objects in this area and damage boats based on how close they are to the center. Golden deals more damage.
  • Long shot - Travels up to 4 tiles and deals 50% damage (75% golden).

Extra Settings

  • Respawn 2pt Flags - When a 2 point flag is taken home, should it respawn back at its starting point or vanish?
  • Stealable flags on sink - If enabled, a carried flag drops without a team when you sink. The first team to touch it again can then claim it for points. This only matters for 2 point flags as 1 point flags are already free for all.
