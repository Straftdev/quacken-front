Capture the flag!

This game mode has two main variations. Either take the flags back to your spawn zone, or plant them in the enemy base.

The flags

The flags here work different than blockade flags. You pick them up by driving over them and drop them by using the flag maneuver. 1 point flags have to be dropped in the 8 tiles surrounding a beacon (3 point flag) matching your team color to earn points for your team. 2 point flags need to be taken back to your spawn zone. 3 point flags cannot be picked up.

New Boats

  • Healer - Healing shot, heal self.
  • Builder - Place rock, in-place turn.
  • Bomber - Place bomb, torpedo.
  • Sniper - Long shot, double forward.

New Maneuvers

  • Healing shot - Heals 25% (50% golden)
  • Heal self - Heals 25% (50% golden)
  • Place rock - Places a short rock on an empty tile next to the boat. Golden can drop on a wind as well. If you hit a boat, it sinks instantly.
  • Place bomb - Place a bomb similar to kraken hunt, but it moves with winds and whirls. Deals 25% damage (50% golden).
  • Torpedo - Fires a torpedo from the front of your ship which travels 2 tiles and removes short rocks in a 3x3 area. It will also clear other objects in this area and damage boats based on how close they are to the center. Golden deals more damage.
  • Long shot - Travels up to 4 tiles and deals 25% damage (50% golden).
