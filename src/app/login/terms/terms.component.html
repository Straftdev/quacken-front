Terms of Use

Overview

Please keep in mind, this is only a game. As such, the following rules are meant mainly as guidelines to keep the environment friendly and welcoming. As long as you are not actively trying to ruin someone's day, you will most likely be alright. The punishments for the (hopefully) rare offenses severe enough to demand Further action include temporary bans and global mute. You will never be permanently banned.

Policies

Annoying People

You are provided with several tools to deal with the people you wish would just go away. To hide all messages from a user, click their name in chat and select 'block'. A blocked player will not be able to join a lobby you own. /kick <name> can be used to eject the targeted player from the current lobby if you own it. Used together, the offending player will effectively be "gone".

Considering the options, it should be easy to see why being annoying is not a crime in itself. If, however, you witness or are targeted by something that ignoring is not an option, you are advised to contact a moderator who will be able to review the situation and determine if further action is appropriate.

Botting

If you think it is fun to tell a computer to play the game for you, go for it! The only thing we ask is that you turn "bot mode" on in your user settings when you bot to skip statistic counting. This keeps the competitive scene "clean", though if there is demand, we may include separate statistics for bots so you can compete there instead. Failure to enable bot mode may lead to your statistics being erased.

Real Money Trades

We do not provide ANY insurance or tools to assist in trades that extend beyond the boundaries of the game. Trade at your own risk. That being said, we will not punish you for doing so. It is entirely at your own discretion.
